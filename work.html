WORK

INVESTMENT RESEARCH & ANALYTICS


Our Investment Research & Analytics team engages in in-depth analysis of sustainable investments on a full spectrum of investment alternatives - from stocks, mutual funds and ETFs, to private equity, micro-finance and impact investments.
Through our in-house expertise in data modelling and analytics, we model and evaluate complex investment risk factors, fundamental and environmental factors, ESG data, and other data intelligence, and combine this work into leading edge data analytics tools and research, offering comprehensive and intelligent investment resources for responsible investors and institutions.



SUSTAINABLE INVESTMENTS RESEARCH

We develop proprietary multi-factor models and data analytic tools, and produce research based on these proprietary models and findings, offering sophisticated sustainable investments research & analysis, to help socially & environmentally responsible investors intelligently evaluate their alternatives.

Our core Sustainable Investment Research products include:

  • Our Sustainable Investment Score - Our proprietary evaluation framework for investments, based on social, environmental, and economic factors, and other data intelligence.
  • Our Sustainable Investment Alternatives research reports offers objective and insightful evaluations of socially responsible, ethical, and green investment alternatives, for both individuals and institutions.
  • Our Sustainable Model Portfolios and Sustainable Stock Lists are based on our proprietary analytics and models, offering unique sustainable portfolio alternatives and portfolio construction intelligence.

DUE DILIGENCE & INVESTMENT PORTFOLIO EVALUATIONS

We offer professional unbiased third-party due diligence and investment assessment services for specific investment opportunities, as well as investment manager performance and evaluation of total investment portfolios. Our investment due diligence services include objective investment portfolio and investment manager evaluations, portfolio risk factor assessments, and ongoing portfolio monitoring services.

Our core Due Diligence & Investment Portfolio Evaluation products include:

  • Our Investment Portfolio Evaluation report comprehensively evaluates investment and risk metrics, including fundamental factors, asset allocation, industry risk exposure, currency risk exposure, as well as ESG and key environmental factors, including a portfolio's Carbon Intensity Profile.
  • Our Carbon Footprint Assessment report uncovers the collective carbon intensity of an investment fund or portfolio and compares this against various benchmarks, identifies where its carbon concentrations lie, provides solid alternatives for portfolio de-carbinization, and offers solution for reallocating capital towards lower emitting solutions to lessen its environmental footprint.
